Some miscellaneous songs and albums which are unavailable on Spotify

2026-03-03

I recently shared a link to my Spotify account on my website, as I’m sure you noticed in the navigation bar. It dawned on me afterwards that Spotify is missing many of my favorite songs and albums - most of my Spotify playlists feel like incomplete representations of my taste as a result - so I thought to share some miscellaneous favorites with you all which, to the best of my knowledge, aren’t available on Spotify.

I previously worked at Spotify as a contractor, as many of you know, but, as somebody who initially attended university with intentions to become an artist, I have some mixed thoughts and feelings about the company. Maybe I will share my thoughts on how Spotify has shaped the music industry and artist’s careers some other time, or maybe I’ll share more about why I decided not to pursue a creative career . . .

For now, however, I hope you all find something you like below.
